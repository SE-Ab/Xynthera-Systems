Common questions

Good questions
from people like you.

How much does a typical project cost?

It depends on scope, but most small business projects land between ₹1,50,000 and ₹8,00,000. We give you a fixed quote upfront — no hourly surprises. A simple website is closer to the lower end; a full booking platform with mobile apps is closer to the upper end.

How long does it take to build something?

An MVP typically ships in 4–8 weeks. Larger systems take 2–4 months. We break everything into weekly milestones so you see working software from week two, not at the end.

Do you work with businesses across India?

Yes. We serve clients from Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, and beyond — in English or Hindi, whichever you prefer. Calls happen on your schedule (IST), and we’re always just a WhatsApp message away.

What happens after the project ships?

We offer optional monthly support plans starting at ₹15,000/month — covering hosting, monitoring, small changes, and priority bug fixes. If you want to take it fully in-house, we hand over clean, documented code.

Do I own the code you write?

100%. Every line of code, every design file, every database — yours on day one, delivered through your own GitHub or GitLab. No vendor lock-in, ever.

Can you work with an existing system?

Absolutely. We take over, audit, and improve existing codebases regularly. If your previous developer left you stuck, we can usually get you unstuck within a week.
